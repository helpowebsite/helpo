Frequently Asked Questions

Helpo is a note-taking website designed to help students to be more productive with their studies. The website offers 4 main features which are Notes, Whiteboard, Pomodoro Timer, and calendar.


- First press the '+' button to start adding a note

- Next, add a title to your note

- Then you can add description to your notes


- In the left panel, there are tools that you can use to use the whiteboard

- By clicking Shapes, you can add shapes to the whiteboard

- Fill Color lets you fill the hollow shapes with your desired color

- Brush can be used to draw, while Eraser can be used to
remove everything drawn on the canvas

- You can change the size of the brush and eraser by dragging the blue slider from left to right and vice versa

- You can also choose your own colors

- By clicking clear canvas, everything that's written or drawn will be erased and reset back into a white blank canvas

- you can download the image of your sketches by clicking save image


- First, click add task. Then add the name of the task you are currently working on

- Then add the number of estimated pomodoros your task will take.

- The pomodoro is fixed to 25 mins. By clicking start, the timer will start counting down until it finishes

- As the countdown finishes, it will add +1 to the number of estimated pomodoros you input. With this you can track the number of pomodoros you have used until it reaches the amount
of estimated number you input

- You can take short or long breaks as well. Short breaks reach 5 mins and long breaks reach a total of 15 mins


- The calendar is mainly used to track events and date

- By clicking the '+' sign at the bottom right it will require you to add the name of the event and the time the event starts and ends

- The time is formatted into a 24-hour, so if you input 18:00 as the time it translate as 6PM in a 12-hour format

- By clicking at the event displayed it will ask you to delete the said event if you confirm it to be deleted.

- You can also look at other years by clicking the arrows

- Clicking 'Today' will redirect you to the present date
